# This is fun

### A game similar to apples to apples or cards against humanity

#### Gameplay
- All users get 7 'white' cards
- A user is designated the 'selector' for a round
- The selector then draws a 'black' card to be used for the round
- All other users excluding the selector pick a white card from their 7 white cards for the selector to then choose
- Selector chooses a winner who then gets 1 point
- Game ends when a certain amount of points have been reached

#### Technical considerations
 - Must be ablt to import custom white and black cards
 - Users need to be able to join into a game
 - White cards and black cards can only be used once per game
